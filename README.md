# New-york-Coffee-Shop-s-revenue-analysis
 This Repo is about the revenue and growth of new york coffee shop's. I do Deep data analysis and also apply machine learning models
 
# 📊 Key Findings

## 💰 Revenue & Growth

Total revenue: $698,812 with an average transaction value of $4.69
Revenue grew steadily every month, with June 2023 being the best month — a clear upward trajectory across the entire 6-month period
The month-over-month growth chart shows consistent positive growth (no down months), suggesting strong seasonal tailwinds heading into summer

## 🏪 Store Performance

Hell's Kitchen is the top-performing store by total revenue, closely followed by Astoria and Lower Manhattan
All three stores show similar revenue patterns — the brand is relatively consistent across locations, which is a sign of operational maturity

## ☕ Product Insights

Coffee dominates with ~40%+ of total revenue, followed by Tea as a distant second
Barista Espresso is the single highest-revenue product type — it's the clear hero SKU
Categories like Packaged Chocolate and Branded items contribute minimal revenue and may be candidates for shelf-space reconsideration

## ⏰ Traffic Patterns

Peak hour is 10:00 AM, with a strong morning rush between 8 AM–11 AM — classic coffee shop behaviour
The heatmap confirms traffic is heaviest Monday through Friday mornings, with weekends showing a flatter, more spread-out pattern
Evenings after 6 PM are very quiet — there may be an opportunity (or a cost-saving case) there

## 📅 Day of Week

Weekdays drive the bulk of revenue, consistent with a commuter/office crowd customer base
Fridays and Thursdays tend to be slightly stronger than early-week days
